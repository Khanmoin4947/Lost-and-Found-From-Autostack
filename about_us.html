About Us

Our mission

This Lost & Found service exists to reconnect people with the belongings that matter to them most. By providing a simple, focused space for reporting lost and found items, we help reduce stress and speed up reunions.

How it works

  1. Report items you have lost or found using the Report page.
  2. Browse the Items page to look for matches.
  3. Use the contact details on each item card to reach out.
  4. Arrange a safe handover in a public place.
  5. Mark the item as resolved (if functionality is later added).

Privacy & safety

Only share contact details you are comfortable making visible. When meeting someone to exchange an item, choose a well-lit public location and, if possible, bring another person with you.
